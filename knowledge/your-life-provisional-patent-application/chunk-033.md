## **Embodiment 9: Community‑Driven Knowledge Expansion**

In this embodiment, the community layer contributes to a shared knowledge graph.

### Components
- Community posts  
- Tags and topics  
- Semantic similarity clusters  
- Cross‑tenant references  

### Operation
- Community content is indexed separately from private content.  
- Tenants may opt‑in to allow their public content to influence community recommendations.  
- The system may generate community‑wide insights.

This embodiment supports discovery and collective intelligence.

## **Embodiment 10: Public Agent as a Transactional Interface**

In this embodiment, the public agent performs actions on behalf of the tenant, such as:

- scheduling meetings  
- processing orders  
- answering inquiries  
- directing users to external platforms  

### Operation
- The public agent integrates with third‑party APIs.  
- Actions are logged and auditable.  
- The agent operates within strict permission boundaries.

This embodiment transforms the public agent into a digital concierge.
